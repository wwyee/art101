Pseudocode: Making a Pancake

  1. Gather ingredients:
    • Flour
    • Eggs
    • Milk
    • Sugar (optional)
    • Salt
    • Butter or oil (for cooking)
  2. In a bowl, add:
    • Flour
    • Eggs
    • Milk
    • Sugar (if using)
    • A pinch of salt
  3. Mix ingredients until a smooth batter forms
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat
  5. Add a small amount of butter or oil to the pan
  6. Pour some batter into the pan
  7. Cook until bubbles form and edges look set
  8. Flip the pancake
  9. Cook the other side until golden brown
  10. Remove pancake from pan
  11. Repeat steps 6â€“10 for remaining batter
  12. Serve with desired toppings (e.g., syrup, fruit, butter)
